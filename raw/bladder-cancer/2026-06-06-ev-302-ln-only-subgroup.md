# EV-302 Lymph-Node-Only Subgroup Analysis

**Source URL:** https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12779424/  
**Collected:** 2026-06-06  
**Published:** 2025-12-22

---

## Key data (EV-302 / KEYNOTE-A39)

- Total randomized: 886 (EVP 442 vs chemo 444)
- LN-only disease: 207 patients (24%) — 103 EVP, 104 chemo

### LN-only subgroup efficacy

| Endpoint | EV+P | Chemotherapy |
|----------|------|--------------|
| ORR | 77.5% | 53.4% |
| CR rate | 50.0% | 26.2% |
| Median PFS | 22.1 mo | 8.3 mo (HR 0.47) |
| Median OS | NR | 24.4 mo (HR 0.51) |

### Overall population (reference)

- Median PFS: 12.5 vs 6.3 mo (HR 0.48)
- Median OS: 33.8 vs 15.9 mo (HR 0.51)
- ORR: 67.5% vs 44.2%

**Clinical note:** LN-only in EV-302 = metastatic disease without visceral metastases, NOT cN1 cM0 perioperative MIBC.
