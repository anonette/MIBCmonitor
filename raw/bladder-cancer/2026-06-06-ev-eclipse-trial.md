# EV-ECLIPSE — Neoadjuvant EV+pembro in Node-Positive MIBC

**Source URL:** https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT05239624  
**Collected:** 2026-06-06  
**Published:** Unknown (trial registry)

---

## Trial identity

- NCT05239624 / MSK 21-316
- Phase 2, Memorial Sloan Kettering sponsor
- Enrollment: ~23 (estimated)
- Status: Recruiting (est. completion Jun 2027)

## Eligibility (relevant to target patient)

- cT2-T4 N1-N3 M0 OR cT1 N2-N3 M0
- MIBC confirmed on TURBT; high-grade urothelial
- Node positivity: FNA/biopsy preferred; or imaging ≥1.0 cm short axis
- Candidate for radical cystectomy
- Cisplatin eligible AND ineligible

## Treatment

- Neoadjuvant: EV + pembrolizumab
- Surgery: radical cystectomy
- Adjuvant: pembrolizumab monotherapy (11 cycles)

## Primary endpoint

- Pathologic complete response (pCR) rate

**Match to target profile:** Near-perfect (cT2 cN1 cM0 perioperative)
