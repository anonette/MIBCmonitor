# KEYNOTE-905 / EV-303 — Perioperative EV+pembro in Cisplatin-Ineligible MIBC

**Source URL:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41707170/  
**Collected:** 2026-06-06  
**Published:** 2025 (NEJM)

---

## Design

- Phase 3, open-label; N=344 (170 EV+pembro, 174 control)
- Population: MIBC cisplatin-ineligible or declined; T2-T4aN0M0 or T1-T4aN1M0
- Baseline: T2N0 18%; T3/T4aN0 77%; T1-T4aN1 4.9% (~17 patients)

## Results (median follow-up 25.6 mo)

| Endpoint | EV+pembro | Control |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| 2-y EFS | 74.7% | 39.4% (HR 0.40) |
| 2-y OS | 79.7% | 63.1% (HR 0.50) |
| pCR | 57.1% | 8.6% (diff 48.3%) |
| Surgery rate | 87.6% | 89.7% |
| Grade ≥3 AE | 71.3% | 45.9% |
