# Zargar-Shoshtar 2016 — Multi-Institutional cN+ MIBC Induction Chemotherapy

**Source:** J Urol 2016;195:53-9  
**Source URL:** https://www.auajournals.org/doi/10.1016/j.juro.2015.02.2296  
**Collected:** 2026-06-07

---

## Population

- 304 patients with cT1-T4a **cN1-N3** M0 urothelial carcinoma (of 1618 in database)
- 19 North American and European centers, 2000-2013
- Chemo: MVAC 42%, GC 43%, other 15%

## Pathologic outcomes

| Outcome | Rate |
|---------|------|
| pN0 (overall) | 48% |
| pN0 in cN1 | 56% |
| pN0 in cN2 | 39% |
| pN0 in cN3 | 39% |
| pCR (≤pT1N0) | 14.5% |

## Survival (median OS)

- ≤pT1N0: not reached
- pT2-4aN0: 45 months
- pTxN1-3: 14 months

**Conclusion:** Complete pathologic nodal response achievable in substantial proportion of cN+ patients; best outcomes with cisplatin regimens, negative margins, and pN0.
